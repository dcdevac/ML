Intro¶

This sheet loads in models trained on the 15 million observations. Compared with sheet 1, I actually added and removed some features - I found a 3rd party Kernel that had distance to certain points and used that in place of the flags for airport etc. I also removed a number of features that were very low on the feature importance analysis
